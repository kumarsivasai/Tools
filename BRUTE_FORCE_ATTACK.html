Intro

Brute force is a method used to crack passwords or encryption keys by systematically trying all possible combinations until the correct one is found. It's one of the simplest forms of attack in cybersecurity but can be very time-consuming depending on the password's strength. Credential stuffing uses leaked username-password pairs to gain access. Brute force is slowed by long, complex passwords, secure hashing, and rate-limiting systems.
